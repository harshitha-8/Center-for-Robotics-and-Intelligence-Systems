About The Center for Robotics & Intelligence Systems (CERIS)

CERIS operates at the intersection of AI, autonomous systems, and the future of agriculture. Our mission is to transform precision farming from mere data collection to true field-level cognition. We envision a future of sustainable agriculture powered by embodied intelligence, where autonomous systems provide growers with deep agronomic insights.

Our unique approach involves building aerial systems from the ground up, integrating computer vision for perception with Large Language Models (LLMs) for reasoning. We are creating autonomous tools that truly understand and interact with the agricultural ecosystem.
